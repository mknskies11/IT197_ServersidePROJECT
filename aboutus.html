Get to know Monochrome:

ABOUT US

Imagine mornings where your wardrobe becomes your canvas, a world of black and white where each piece complements the other. Our garments are designed to harmonize seamlessly, allowing you to orchestrate a symphony of looks with a single note.

Monochrome is an elegant composition, a carefully curated wardrobe where black and white notes weave together to create a timeless symphony of style. We transcend fleeting trends, offering a haven for those who cherish the enduring allure of monochrome.

Monochrome HQ

At LA, USA

Monochrome Fashion isn't just a clothing company, it's a Los Angeles story written in 2022. Our headquarters, a stunning interplay of light and shadow in the heart of LA, embodies the very essence of our brand: where West Coast cool meets enduring monochrome style.

Step inside our headquarters and you'll find a space that reflects the philosophy woven into every stitch of our clothing. Think exposed brick walls juxtaposed with sleek, modern furniture. Abundant natural light floods the studio, highlighting the meticulous craftsmanship of our black and white collections. It's a space that celebrates the beauty of contrasts, a testament to the versatility and timeless appeal of black and white fashion.

MEET OUR CEO & FOUNDER

Monochrome's CEO and Founder, Celia Rhodes isn't just an average CEO. A whirlwind of creativity and business acumen, she's become a fashion iconoclast at a young age. She doesn't follow trends, she sets them. Celia saw the endless possibilities within the classic palette of black and white, transforming Monochrome Fashion into a symbol of modern rebellion and timeless elegance. Her designs are as bold as her vision, a fusion of LA's coolness with a touch of the unexpected.

She isn't just building a fashion brand, she's building a monochrome movement. Celia Rhodes is a force to be reckoned with, a leader who doesn't just talk about disrupting the fashion industry, she's doing it, one perfectly tailored black and white masterpiece at a time.
